Create a Motoko actor that functions as a backend for a flashcard application. The actor should expose the following functions:
- Create a flashcard with a question and answer.
- Read a flashcard by its id.
- Update a flashcard by its id.
- Delete a flashcard by its id.
- List all flashcards sorted by id.
- Rate a flashcard in terms of confidence from 1-5.
- List all flashcards sorted by confidence.
