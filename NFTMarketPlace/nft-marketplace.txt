NFT Marketplace for Static NFTs with the following features:

- Users can mint new NFTs with image data and name
- Users can list their NFTs for sale with a price
- Users can purchase listed NFTs from other users
- Users can view all their owned NFTs
- Users can view all listed NFTs in the marketplace
- Users can check if an NFT is listed
- Users can verify ownership of NFTs
- Users can get the original owner of a listed NFT
- Users can get the price of a listed NFTs
