Write a Flash Loan Protocol in Motoko that features:
- Provides instant, uncollateralized loans for a fixed duration.
- Uses a liquidity pool to fund loans and receive repayments.
- Charges a daily interest on borrowed amounts.
- Loans must be repaid before the due date to avoid default.
- Borrowers can only take one loan at a time.
- Loan amounts cannot exceed available liquidity.
- Loan duration is measured in days and calculated precisely.
- Repayment includes principal + interest, returning funds to the pool.
- Borrowers can check their loan details anytime.
- Liquidity pool balance is updated dynamically.
- Ensures fair and transparent lending on the motoko blockchain.
